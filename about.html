Why Choose Us

Our Story

Zaobora Co. Ltd was founded in 2020 with a vision to revolutionize agriculture in Tanzania. Starting in the Southern Highlands, we have grown into a key player in the agricultural sector, known for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community empowerment.

Our journey is driven by the desire to uplift smallholder farmers, improve livelihoods, and contribute to climate resilience.

VISION

To be an agribusiness that places producers, consumers, and the environment at the heart of everything we do.

MISSION

To create and nurture fair and sustainable value chains that contribute to resilient rural livelihoods across Tanzania
