Farmer Advisory and Extension Services

“Empowering Farmers with Smart Agriculture Solutions”

At Zaobora, we revolutionize agricultural practices with innovative mobile app solutions. Our personalized extension services integrate evidence based advice on agroecological techniques and provide access to quality farm inputs to optimize yields and enhance sustainability for Tanzanian smallholder farmers. Our mission is to empower them with the knowledge and resources for sustainable crop yields and resilience in today's dynamic agricultural landscape.

Agroforestry Design and Farmer Training

“Cultivating Sustainability Through Agroforestry”

Environmental sustainability is the cornerstone of our mission. We train farmers to seamlessly integrate trees into their cropping systems, boosting productivity and enhancing climate resilience. Supported by our extensive network of tree nurseries, we provide high-quality seedlings and ongoing guidance to guarantee success. Our innovative agroforestry approach not only secures a sustainable future for farmers but also contributes to a healthier planet for all.

Market Linkages for Farmers

“Connecting Farmers to Prosperous Markets”

Zaobora believes that farmers should reap the full rewards of their hard work. We bridge the gap between farmers and buyers by direct market linkages, ensuring fair prices and better market access. Beyond traditional markets, we've introduced farmers to carbon markets, unlocking new income opportunities and adding value to their efforts. By expanding market horizons, we're transforming livelihoods and fueling economic growth in rural communities.

Agribusiness Consultancy

“Maximize Your Agricultural Investments with Expert Insight”

Ready to invest in agriculture with confidence? Zaobora's Agribusiness Consultancy empowers investors to achieve maximum returns in the flourishing agricultural sector. Specializing in profitable ventures across crop production, poultry, and small ruminants, we offer unrivaled expertise in Tanzania. Our tailored strategies are aligned with market trends, ensuring sustainable growth and profitability. Whether you're diversifying your portfolio or exploring new sectors, we provide the insights and guidance to turn your investment into a thriving success. Partner with us to navigate the local landscape and capitalize on results-driven solutions.
