Our Services

Boosting productivity and sustainability for farmers

Smart Agriculture and Precision Farming Services

Our personalized extension services deliver evidence-based agroecological advice, access to certified farm inputs, and precision farming techniques. These solutions help smallholder farmers in Tanzania optimize yields, boost productivity, and achieve sustainable agriculture.

Sustainable Agroforestry and Climate Resilience Solutions

We train farmers in agroforestry systems, integrating tree crops for enhanced climate resilience, soil fertility, and biodiversity conservation. Through our network of tree nurseries, we provide high-quality seedlings to ensure long-term success in sustainable farming practices.

Market Linkage and Carbon Credit Opportunities for Farmers

We connect farmers with buyers to ensure fair prices and better market access. Our innovative approach also opens up new income streams through carbon markets, helping farmers thrive financially.

Agribusiness Consultancy and Investment Planning

Looking to invest in agriculture? Our consultancy provides tailored strategies to maximize returns, specializing in profitable ventures across crop production, poultry, and small ruminants in the Southern Highlands.
